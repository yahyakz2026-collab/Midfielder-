# MID8 — Midfielder Training

Progressive 8-week training app to develop from Wingback into a Box-to-Box Midfielder.

**Features:**
- Flexible daily sessions (Pitch, Gym, Home, Cone, Recovery, Mental)
- Drill checklists with progress
- Interval timer with presets
- Hydration + weekly goals tracker
- Skill progress & session history
- Fully works offline after first visit

Open `index.html` in your browser or install as a PWA on your phone.

Made for semi-pro and ambitious players balancing shifts/training.
